Welcome to my world, filtered through a camera and a bit of chaos.

Hey! I’m Lex Romo — part cyber nerd, part cloud wrangler, and part guy-who-always-has-a-camera-on-him. I work in tech by day, but when I’m not fighting off digital threats, I’m out chasing good light and better coffee. I’ve got a photo blog where I post snaps of whatever catches my eye — moody skies, chill street scenes, the occasional blurry bird that almost looked majestic. It’s kind of like my digital diary, but prettier (and with fewer spelling mistakes). If you’re into tech, photography, or just vibing with someone who’s trying to find beauty in the chaos, you’re in the right place.
